My Story

  • Maker
  • Researcher
  • Entrepreneur
  • Optimist

I'm a Senior AI Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs, where I lead research on building a generative World Model to power the next generation of immersive experiences — enabling the recreation of memories and environments from everyday photos and casual inputs.

I completed my PhD at Berkeley advised by Professor Angjoo Kanazawa, where I focused on extending 3D reconstruction (and generation) to casual captures — mobile phone videos, TV shows, and cartoons. You can find my PhD dissertation talk recording here!

Originally from a small town in Wisconsin, I'm passionate about helping others with technological solutions. Previously I was an undergraduate and Master's student at MIT, where I was fortunate to work with and learn from Professor Antonio Torralba.
